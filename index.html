Welcome

I am a Heising-Simons Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of California, Santa Barbara. My research focuses on the quantum nature of black holes, the information paradox, and extremely compact objects, with an emphasis on connecting high-energy theory to astrophysical observations and identifying observable signatures of quantum gravitational effects near black holes.
